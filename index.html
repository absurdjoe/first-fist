How to Use the App

  1. Secure your phone firmly in your hand or strap it tightly to your arm using an armband.
  2. Make sure the phone does not move independently while punching.
  3. Get into a proper stance and throw a clean, controlled punch.
  4. Instantly view your punch force score on the screen.

Get the Best Out of Every Punch

  • Use a tight grip or a secure armband to prevent phone movement.
  • Keep your punches clean and controlled for accurate readings.
  • Maintain proper form—balance, stance, and follow-through matter.
  • Avoid random swinging; consistent technique gives better tracking.
  • Start slow and increase power gradually to track real improvement.

Train Safe ⚠️

  • Always ensure your phone is securely held or strapped before punching.
  • Do not punch hard surfaces while holding your phone.
  • Avoid overexertion—build intensity gradually.
  • Keep a safe distance from people and objects.
  • Stop immediately if you feel discomfort or strain.
  • Use protective gear (like hand wraps) if training intensely.

How It Works

This app uses your phone’s built-in accelerometer to measure motion and speed during your punch. When you punch, the sensor detects how fast and forcefully your hand moves. The app then converts this movement into a punch force score using smart calculations. The cleaner and more controlled your punch, the more accurate your result will be.
